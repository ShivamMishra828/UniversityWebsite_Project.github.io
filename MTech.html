MTech In EE (Power System)

Masters of Technology in Power Systems Engineering or MTech Power Systems Engineering is a 2- year professional Electrical Engineering course that offers specialization in power systems. The course comprises state of art techniques such as Stability Evaluation Planning, Industrial Electronics and Controls, Reliability and Forecasting, Power Electronics and Drives, Wind and Solar Energy Electric Conversion Systems, System Analysis of Power, etc,.The subjects covered during the course programme: Advanced Engineering Mathematics, Advanced Power System Analysis, High Voltage Transmission, Power System Operation & Control, Power System Instrumentation, Advanced Topics in Micro Processors.

MTech In Information and Technology

MTech Information Technology is a 2-Year postgraduate degree program for candidates who are interested in computer programming, software engineering, wireless communication, intelligent systems, etc.The course provides a vast study of various programming languages, components of design, development, implementation, support, and management of computer-based IT systems.During this course study, the students will learn installing computer applications, designing complex computer network systems, databases, etc.

MTech In CE (Structure and Foundation)

It is a 2-year postgraduate program in the field of civil engineering with specialization in structural engineering. The program is spread over four semesters, each semester being of six months. The first two semesters focus on the theory part along with seminars, workshops, group discussions on current developments in the field as well as laboratory work. The final two semesters are more about seminars and projects. There are also industry visits by the students during the final year of their study as well as industry experts visiting the college and addressing the students on various aspects of structural engineering.

MTech In ME (Thermal Science Engineering)

MTech Thermal engineering is a sub-discipline of mechanical engineering concerned with the movement and transmission of heat energy. Energy can be converted into different types or moved across two mediums. It entails the investigation of design systems that use thermodynamics to deliver heating or cooling by transferring heat or energy through liquids and gases. It aids in understanding how these systems are built and doing tests to verify appropriate functionality. Candidates enrolled in the programme receive training in design, development, and simulation, as well as the opportunity to explore new topics in Thermal Energy Systems and related sciences.

MTech In ME (Automation & Robotics)

M.E. Automation and Robotics or Master of Engineering in Automation and Robotics is a two-year postgraduate Mechanical engineering program. Automation and Robotics focus on the design of modern Robotic systems. It is the area of computer-controlled technology and deals with the construction, operation, structural disposition, manufacture, and application of robots. Automation and Robotics Engineering is a rapidly growing field. The course is career orienting in nature that opens many opportunities in various fields.
